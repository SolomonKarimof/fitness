Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym's operating hours?
Our gym is open from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, Monday through Sunday.
Do I need a membership to use the gym?
Yes, a membership is required to access the gym facilities.
Are there personal trainers available?
Yes, we have certified personal trainers available for sessions. Please inquire at the front desk for more details.
What types of equipment do you have?
We offer a wide range of equipment including treadmills, stationary bikes, free weights, and resistance machines.
Do you offer group fitness classes?
Yes, we offer a variety of group fitness classes including yoga, Zumba, Pilates, and more. Check our schedule for the latest classes.
